# Known bugs & typos

This page contains a list of known typos. It is currently unknown whether any typos are intentional.

## Bugs

Some bugs were present in versions of the code that have been fixed in the meantime. This indicates that 
visual glitches etc. were most likely not intentional and are a priority for the development team.

## Typos

The misspellings seem to persist after the 26th of May update, which means they could still be significant.

- The webpage contains the word `paitent`at least two times, likely being a misspelling of patient.
- There is a misspelling in the text you get when opening the [capslock doc](files/capslock_doc.md)
- `Selene'Storage` and `comprims` in the [capslock doc](files/capslock_doc.md)
